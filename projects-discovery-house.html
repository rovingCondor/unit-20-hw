DISCOVERY HOUSE WEBSITE REDESIGN

Project Overview

Analyze and propose solutions for the Discovery House website to give visitors a more informative and immersive experience. Improvements will have a dual goal of providing domestic abuse survivors with critical information while providing information on how to donate and support this non-profit organization.

Problem

The Discovery House website is challenged to provide users with quick and easy to access information about their organization and contribution to the community.

Solution

Rethink the content strategy, improve the content hierarchy and capitalize on opportunities to enhance user engagement.

Roles

  • UX Design
  • UI Design
  • UX Research
  • UX Testing
