FLOK MOBILE TRAVEL APP

Project Overview

Early development of Flok, a mobile travel app with a focus on UX research, ideation, testing & prototyping.

Problem

In the post-pandemic world, people are encountering a number of pain points while planning travel.

Solution

Develop a mobile app that offers the user exploration, chat and polling features to help make their next trip more of a cohesive experience
