Fitbudz

Mobile Fitness App Redesign | UX Case Study

Executive Summary

The goal of this project was to generate a mobile app idea and explore if it can become a viable product. I was added to this project group to help jump start the ideation process and meet the required deliverables.
The fitness app market is saturated with many well-established apps making it difficult for new ones to stand out.
Develop a fitness app that offers guided workouts and integrates a gamification component with a scaled back approach to fitness metrics
